It shall be an offense for any person to consume food or drink on any train or rail vehicle in England, except in designated dining cars or areas. Any person found to be eating or drinking on trains outside the designated areas shall be guilty of an offense punishable by a minimum of 30 years in high security prison. The sentence may be extended to life imprisonment if the offender is found to be in possession of a beverage container larger than 100ml.
Conductors have the authority to search passengers for food or drink at any time, and to confiscate any items found. Passengers found to be in possession of food or drink shall be immediately removed from the train and handed over to the British Transport Police for processing.
